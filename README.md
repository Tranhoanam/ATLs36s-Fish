## Gameplay Guide Instructions
1. **Getting Started**:  Start by creating an account and selecting your first fish.
2. **Exploring**:  Explore different areas to find various species of fish.
3. **Using Bait**:  Different baits attract different fish. Experiment with various types!
4. **Upgrading Gear**:  Improve your gear to increase your chances of catching rare fish.
5. **Completing Challenges**:  Participate in daily and weekly challenges for special rewards.

Good luck and happy fishing!
